Shrimp Tempura

Dish Details

Description:
Crispy battered shrimp served with a soy dipping sauce.

Ingredients:
Shrimp, tempura batter (flour, cornstarch, egg), soy sauce, sugar, mirin, dashi, ginger.

Allergens:
Contains shellfish, gluten, egg, soy.

Meat Content:
Contains shrimp.
Gluten-Free: No.

Macros (per serving):
Calories: 400 kcal
Protein: 25g
Carbohydrates: 30g
Fat: 22g
Fiber: 2g
Sugar: 5g
