Tempura Udon Noodles

Dish Details

Description:
A quick and satisfying meal of thick, chewy udon noodles in a savory, umami-rich broth with tempura flavor. Includes dried vegetables, fish cake slices, and crunchy tempura flakes for added texture.

Ingredients:
Udon noodles (wheat flour, salt), tempura flakes (wheat, palm oil), fish cake, dehydrated vegetables (green onion, carrot), soy sauce powder, bonito extract, seasoning powder (flavor enhancers, salt, sugar, spices).

Allergens:
Contains gluten, soy, fish.
May contain traces of eggs, milk, crustaceans, and sesame.

Meat Content:
No meat content. Fish-based flavor.
Gluten-Free: No.

Macros (per serving):
Calories: 450 kcal
Protein: 9g
Carbohydrates: 60g
Fat: 18g
Fiber: 3g
Sugar: 4g
