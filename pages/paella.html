Seafood Paella

Dish Details

Description:
A traditional Spanish rice dish infused with saffron and simmered in a rich seafood broth. Topped with mussels, shrimp, bell peppers, and a fresh lemon wedge.

Ingredients:
Bomba rice, mussels, shrimp, bell peppers, green beans, tomatoes, garlic, onion, olive oil, saffron, paprika, seafood broth, lemon.

Allergens:
Contains shellfish.

Contains seafood (mussels, shrimp).
