Spaghetti with Meatballs & Sausage

Dish Details

Description:
A hearty Italian classic featuring al dente spaghetti topped with slow-simmered marinara sauce, homemade beef meatballs, and grilled Italian sausage links.

Ingredients:
Spaghetti (contains gluten), beef meatballs (beef, breadcrumbs, egg, parsley, spices), Italian sausage (pork), tomato sauce, garlic, onion, basil, olive oil, salt, pepper, parmesan (optional).

Allergens:
Contains gluten, egg, possibly dairy (if cheese is added).

Meat Content:
Contains beef and pork.
Gluten-Free: No.

Macros (per serving):
Calories: 690 kcal
Protein: 35g
Carbohydrates: 55g
Fat: 35g
Fiber: 5g
Sugar: 6g
