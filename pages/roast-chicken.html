Herb Roasted Chicken

Dish Details

Description:
Juicy whole chicken, roasted to perfection and seasoned with rosemary, thyme, and garlic. Served on a bed of roasted root vegetables and garnished with lemon wedges.

Ingredients:
Whole chicken, olive oil, rosemary, thyme, garlic, salt, pepper, potatoes, carrots, onions, lemon.

Allergens:
None.

Meat Content:
Contains chicken.
Gluten-Free: Yes.
