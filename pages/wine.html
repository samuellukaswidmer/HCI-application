Wine Selection

Recommended Wines

Chardonnay (White):
A rich and creamy white wine with hints of vanilla and butter — pairs well with Caesar Salad and Risotto.

Merlot (Red):
A smooth and fruity red wine — complements pasta like Spaghetti Bolognese beautifully.

Sauvignon Blanc (White):
Crisp and refreshing — excellent with shrimp dishes like Tempura.

Alcohol Content:
Typically ranges between 12–14% ABV.

Allergens:
Some wines may contain sulfites.
